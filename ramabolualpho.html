About Us

Founded in 2015, our firm specializes in transforming homes and offices into inspiring environments. Our mission is to create spaces that tell a story and reflect the people who live or work in them.

Tankiso 'Alpho' Ramabolu, Founder

As the founder of our interior design firm, Alpho Ramabolu combines creativity with a deep understanding of design principles to bring innovative ideas to life. With a passion for transforming spaces, Alpho leads the team in delivering unique and personalized designs that reflect the individuality of each client. His vision and dedication to quality ensure that every project not only meets but exceeds client expectations. Alpho’s commitment to sustainable practices and attention to detail set the foundation for a design philosophy that prioritizes both beauty and functionality.

Lebohang Mochesane, Creative Director

Our Creative Director leads the design team, shaping the aesthetic vision for each project. They collaborate closely with clients to ensure that every design aligns with their expectations, while also making key decisions on materials, colors, and overall design direction.

Kamohelo Hoala, Project Manager

The Project Manager plays a crucial role in keeping projects on track. They coordinate schedules, manage budgets, and facilitate communication between clients and contractors, ensuring that every project is completed on time and to the highest standards.

Motlatsi Mokhobatau, Interior Designer

Our talented Interior Designer works directly with clients to create beautiful and functional spaces. They develop design concepts, select furnishings, and choose finishes that enhance the aesthetic and functionality of each environment, turning visions into reality.

Mohau Mpeta

The Marketing Specialist is responsible for promoting our firm and connecting with potential clients. They develop marketing strategies, manage our online presence, and analyze market trends to keep our brand fresh and appealing, ensuring we reach those who seek exceptional design services.
